CampNet is your campus network assistant. It can auto-login to your campus network in the background, so you don’t have to open your browser to login every now and then.

Main features:

====== Auto Management ======
• Auto login
• Auto logout expired connections

====== Usage Monitoring ======
• View usage trends visually
• Estimate monthly fee
• Usage alert

====== Advanced Features ======
• Online devices management
• Multi-account management
• Login other IP

====== General Framework ======
• Use configuration files to adapt campus networks
• Currently supported:
    • Tsinghua University
• Cannot find your school? Submit issues or pull requests at https://github.com/ThomasLee969/CampNet-Configurations !
