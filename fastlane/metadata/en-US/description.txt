CampNet is your full-auto campus network assistant. It can auto-login to your campus network in the background, so you don’t have to open your browser to login every now and then.

Main features:

====== Auto Management ======
• Auto login
• Auto logout expired sessions

====== Usage Monitoring ======
• View usage trends visually
• Today widget
• Usage alert

====== Advanced Features ======
• Online devices management
• Multi-account management
• Login other IPs

====== General Framework ======
• Use configuration files to adapt campus networks
• Currently supported:
    • Tsinghua University
    • Renmin University of China
    • University of Chinese Academy of Sciences
• Cannot find your school? Load your own configuration files!
